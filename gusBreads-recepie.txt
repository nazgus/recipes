Ingredients:
 - 1 ciabatta bread
 - 2 slices of ham
 - 1 mozzarella cheese

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.

 2. Shred desired amount of cheese and chop up the ham into small squares, mix together in a bowl.

 3. Cut the ciabatta bread in half and toast in oven once preheated.

 4. Cover each half with half of the cheese and ham, put back in oven until cheese is nice and toasted.
